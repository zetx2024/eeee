Our Program!

We have a comprehensive and holistic program focusing on multiple forms of mentorship and teaching that will ensure your success!

Global Topics

Our researchers have explored a diverse range of topics across the world from the effectiveness of US pay transparency laws in reducing gender pay gap to the regime influence on poverty reduction in Asian Countries, all of which were researched in support of the UN’s Sustainable Development Goals.
