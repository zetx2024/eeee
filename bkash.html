bKash Mobile Recharge Coupon Offer 2025

Enjoy exciting rewards on your bKash payments with Youth Research Journal Merchants

Campaign Background

bKash along with Youth Research Journal Merchants is launching a campaign where customers will receive a mobile recharge coupon on eligible bKash payments.

Campaign Duration

20 September 2025, 00:00:00 hours to 30 November 2025, 23:59:59 hours

Campaign Modality & Offer

Customer will enjoy BDT 100 Mobile Recharge Coupon on successful bKash payments of BDT 500 or above to selected merchants.

Conditions

  • Limit: Daily - Once | Campaign Period - Twice
  • Coupon Amount: BDT 100
  • Minimum Recharge to Redeem Coupon: BDT 200
  • Coupon Validity: Till the end of the campaign
  • Redemption Channel: App

Eligible Channels

App, USSD, PGW (Checkout, Tokenized, Direct Charge), Pay Bill (App, USSD), Dynamic QR, Visa Pass-through (App, QR, Dynamic QR), Bangla QR.

Eligibility & Rules

  • bKash customers with active accounts and sufficient balance can avail this offer.
  • Coupon will be issued automatically on successful eligible transactions.
  • bKash may disburse coupon manually if failed, within 2 months of campaign.
  • bKash reserves the right to modify or cancel the campaign without notice.
  • Suspicious transactions may lead to cancellation of coupon eligibility.
  • bKash is not responsible for product delivery from merchants.
  • Wrong payments will be addressed after the campaign period based on claims.
