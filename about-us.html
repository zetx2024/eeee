About Youth Research Journal

We aspire to promote young researchers and writers by walking them through the standard procedures of publishing a research article; that is, delivering a manuscript, editing & peer-review, and finally acceptance or rejection. Through our monthly-issues, we hope to bring the hard work of those writers to light while also providing a constant stream of quality, entertaining, and highly scrutinized content for readers hungry for explore. These articles, however, are but an initial spark that encourages readers to go beyond and delve more deeply into their topics of interest on their own, advertising independent research and self-learning.

Our Journey

Since our inception, Youth Research Journal has been dedicated to enhancing the academic experience for students across Bangladesh. We've grown from a small organization to a platform serving over 200 students.

Why Choose Youth Research Journal?

The ultimate vision of YRJ is to create a world in which every student, regardless of background or location, has access to top-notch research and academic programs that unlock their latent potential, promotes their critical thinking and teamwork skills, and feeds their desire to learn. Our ambitious goals depend on a determined effort to increase diversity, improve our educational offerings, increase our influence, and inspire curiosity in future cohorts of researchers through innovative teaching, highly individualized mentorship, and a global presence that is expansive in scope.
